Tips & Guide
Get the most out of KeySense AI
💡
Best Practices
1
Use longer, content-rich text (300+ words) for more accurate keyword extraction.
2
Enable "Filter Stop Words" to remove common words like "the", "is", "and" automatically.
3
Try "Include Phrases" to extract multi-word keyword combinations.
4
Use Smart AI mode for context-aware extraction beyond simple frequency counting.
5
Click any keyword in Results to instantly copy it to your clipboard.
6
Export to CSV for use in spreadsheets, SEO tools, or content planning workflows.
